Common Japanese Phrases

Traveling to a country in which you are not fluent in the language is exciting and scary at the same time. That is why this section of the website has 5 common Japanese pharses.

こんにちは konnichiwa – Hello / Good afternoon!
おはようございます ohayou gozaimasu – Good morning!
すみません sumimasen – Excuse me.
ありがとうございます arigatou gozaimasu – Thank you
もう一度言ってください mou ichidou itte kudasai – Please say it again.
